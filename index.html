Services

Ground shipping

This option of organizing cargo delivery allows you to connect two points “from the sender to the recipient” without additional loading / unloading, which reduces the risk of damage to the cargo, increases the speed of transportation, and also reduces the total cost. The commercial and technological flexibility of road transport services gives them an edge over competing modes of freight transport.

Read more

Railway shipping

One of the longest railway networks in the world belongs to Russia. The shortest transport routes between Europe, the CIS countries and Asia pass through our country. Rail transportation can be performed in any direction by specialized or universal rolling stock.

Read more

Water shipping

Transport services in the field of sea and river cargo transportation are the most environmentally friendly option for cargo delivery. This type of freight transportation is a priority in the exchange of raw materials and goods at the international level.

Read more

Air shipping

Shippers often have situations when it is necessary to deliver in the shortest possible time over long distances. In this case, air transport is indispensable: airplanes, helicopters. This type of cargo transportation is the most expensive, but if the deadlines, as they say, are “running out”, then it is better to use it.
